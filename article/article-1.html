How to create an Aliexpress affiliate account

Create an account and generate affiliate links.

Posted on May 12, 2018

In this article we will cover how to sign up and get started using your Aliexpress affiliate account.

  1. Sign up for an Aliexpress affiliate account
  2. Generate affiliate links
  3. What next?

Sign up for an Aliexpress affiliate account

It is very easy to sign up for an Aliexpress affiliate account once you know how, but it can be slightly confusing if you're goinng in blind. What we will do here is:

  1. Sign up for a regular Aliexpress account
  2. Request that account to be made into an affiliate one

Across both Aliexpress and the Aliexpress affiliate program, there is only one account that a user will hold. There are no separate accounts for regular Ali use and affiliate use. In order to upgrade to an affiliate account with Aliexpress, you will first need to sign up for a regular account (if you don't already have one).

Once we have an affiliate account, it will literally be the same account as a regular one; there isn't a separate sign-up process for affiliate accounts.

Firstly, you'll need to sign up for an Ali account if you haven't got one already. If we visit the Affiliate Portal page you will see two options: Sign in and Register. It seems slightly backwards, but the 'Register' button is to register for an Ali account, not for the affiliate program. To upgrade to an affiliate account you will need to click 'Sign in', where you will be taken to the following page:

Fill in the details for your site however you see fit. Your 'unique affiliate ID' is a number up to 10 digits that you make up. I recommend if you use a password manager to generate and store your ID there. If you don't use a password manager (which you probably should - but that's for another day) then you can use an online random number generator. How you save the number is up to you. Also, if you don't yet have a domain name for your site you will need to buy one. I didn't have anything hosted on my domain when I signed up - it seems to be a manual process for Aliexpress to accept affiliate applications, so just make sure you enter as much information as possible.

Once you have submitted your application there is no notification process once you have been accepted. You will need to sign in to check whether or not you have been accepted. Once you have been accepted you will see this page when you log in. You can now start selling!

Generate affiliate links

To generate an affiliate link, firstly select a product from Aliexpress. I'll try with this watch:

Copy the product url when you have found something you want to generate a link for. Go over to your affiliate portal and navigate to Deep Link URLs.

Paste the URL and remove the latter part of the URL from '.html' onwards.

If you don't do this then you may get an error saying "This URL is not promoted by the Portals currently." If you remove the latter part of the URL and this error persists, then unfortunately the product can't be used as an affiliate link. I find that over 90% of products I search for are supported by the affiliate programme.
